Goeun Kim

I'm a research engineer with a background in biomedical engineering and AI. I received my M.S. from Seoul National University, where I focused on LiDAR-based 3D object detection in hospital environments. My current interests include computer vision, LiDAR perception, and AI systems for autonomous robotics.

Research

I'm interested in computer vision, deep learning, generative AI, and foundation models. Most of my research focuses on analyzing image or 3D sensor data using computer vision networks to develop perception systems for autonomous robots, or designing practical systems powered by foundation models for real-world applications. Some papers are highlighted.

Intelligent Robot-Based Lidar Fusion Integrated Control System Development for Improvement of Medical Environments
Jaekwan Lim, Changsun Ahn, Jun-Min Kim, Goeun Kim
Proceedings of KIIT Conference, 2023

A LiDAR-based integrated control system for managing autonomous hospital robots and ensuring safe interaction with humans and equipment.

A Radiomics-based Unread Cervical Imaging Classification Algorithm
Young Jae Kim, Woong Ju, Kyehyun Nam, Soonyung Kim, Kwang Gi Kim, Go Eun Kim
Journal of Biomedical Engineering Research, 2021

A radiomics-based framework for pre-filtering unreadable cervical images to enhance AI diagnostic reliability and accuracy.
